Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OptimRoute

How does route optimization work?

Our route optimization uses advanced algorithms to calculate the most efficient routes based on multiple factors including distance, traffic patterns, delivery time windows, vehicle capacity, and driver availability. The system considers thousands of possible route combinations to find the optimal solution that minimizes travel time and distance while meeting all delivery constraints.

Can I integrate OptimRoute with my existing systems?

Yes, OptimRoute offers API access and custom integrations that allow you to connect with your existing systems such as order management, CRM, or ERP platforms. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include API access, and the Enterprise plan includes custom integration support to ensure seamless data flow between systems.

How accurate is the real-time tracking?

Our real-time tracking is highly accurate, with location updates every 30 seconds. The system uses a combination of GPS data from driver devices and our proprietary algorithms to provide accurate location information and estimated arrival times. Customers can see the exact location of their delivery and receive automated notifications as the driver approaches.

Is there a limit to the number of stops per route?

The number of stops per route depends on your subscription plan. The Starter plan allows up to 25 stops per route, the Professional plan allows up to 100 stops per route, and the Enterprise plan has no limit on stops per route. Our optimization algorithm can efficiently handle routes with hundreds of stops while still providing optimal results.

Can drivers use their own mobile devices?

Yes, drivers can use their own iOS or Android devices with our mobile app. The app is designed to be lightweight and battery-efficient while providing all the necessary features for drivers to manage their routes, update delivery statuses, and communicate with dispatchers. We also support dedicated GPS devices if preferred.
